Social media is addictive and otherwise problematic. As a result I do not have an instagram or snapchat. However, this creates a dilemma. How can I share life updates with my adoring fans without traditional social media? This wobsite has been created as a solution, allowing me to scream into cyberspace without suffering from the downsides of being exposed to infinite scrolling 'content'.

Make sure to checkout the blag to read things I want to share publicly. After all, what point is oversharing into a series of tubes if nobody is going to look. I intend to post roughly monthly, possibly more often if interesting things happen.

The images section is where I place (hopefully) interesting images I've taken, sorted into events or categories of some sort.

Things I think you should look at after you are done here:

  • XKCD: The webcomic created by Randall Munroe.
  • tom7's website.
  • real world technologies: The likely dead blog of David Kanter.
  • Chips and Cheese: Home of some interesting articles on computer hardware.

Some good Youtube* channels:

  • Miles in Transit: Pushing transit to its limits.
  • PixelPipes: On the history of 3D graphics.
  • HGModernism: I am unsure how to give a blurb for this one.

*Yes, yes I know Youtube is usually social media with infinite scrolling, but if you turn off watch history and only look at the subscriptions tab then it is less bad.
